Welcome to ForestFire!

You have three minutes to try to save as much of the forest as possible.
Your copter carries a limited amount of water. You can refill your tank at the lake (bottom left corner).
Use arrow keys or keypad to move the copter. Hold shift while flying for faster movement.
Press spacebar to drop water, or to fill up watertank when over lake.
ESC will pause or unpause.

Fires will start randomly, and spread if not put out. Fires will eventually burn out when they've destroyed all the trees in a patch.
